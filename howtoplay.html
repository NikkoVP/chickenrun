How To Play

Story

One day on a simple farm, the chicken cage broke down, and all of the chickens ran away except for one. The owner of the farm called you for help. Try to keep the chicken inside the cage until the owner comes back with the tools he needs to fix the cage.

Game rules

The chicken will randomly walk around the broken cage, and every time you try to keep it inside, it will run faster.

Every time you touch the chicken, 1 point will be given to you.

If the chicken got away, game over.

Keys

Press W to go up.
Press S to go down.
